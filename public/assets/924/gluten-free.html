Eating Right Brownie Parfait Sundae
INGREDIENTS
1 box Eating Right Gluten Free Brownie Mix
1/4 cup Safeway Chocolate Chips
1/2 cup Safeway Marshmallows
1 cup Safeway SELECT Vanilla Bean
Ice Cream
2 tbsp Safeway Powdered Sugar
Whipped cream
Prep time: 15 min.
Cook time: 20 min.
Serves: 6
DIRECTIONS
• Preheat oven to 350°F.
• Prepare brownie mix as directed on package, then divide between six oven-safe
 canning jars and place onto baking tray.
• Fill the baking tray with water to reach a quarter of the way up the sides of the jars.
• Bake for 20 minutes.
• Remove from oven and allow to cool.
• To serve, top with chocolate chips, marshmallows and ice cream.
• Garnish with powdered sugar and whipped cream.
